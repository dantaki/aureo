# Methodology
---------------------


## Photography

Stack three cans of food (standard size) and tightly secure a white pillowcase around the top one with a rubber band.

Align the tripod on the table with the cans. Point a light as closely overhead to minimize shadows. 

### Camera settings

* Manual mode
* ISO 125
* F11
* Manually set the white balance according to your lamp
* Manually adjust shutter speed accord to brightness

### Photoshop

Open the RAW file in photoshop. Appy auto settings before opening the photo. 

In photoshop select a square marque 1600px in length. Center the selection over the coin and crop.

Change levels, first on auto then manually adjust

Change image size to 800px and save as JPEG
